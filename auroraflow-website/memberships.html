How it works

Activate your membership

Contact us or request via our booking system. Your credit is added immediately upon activation.

02

Every month a new credit is automatically added to your account for you to use whenever you're ready.

Book online, then apply your member credit at checkout. Need a longer session? Just pay the difference at your discounted rate.

Life happens. Your credits roll over indefinitely, even if your membership is no longer active, you can still use them.
