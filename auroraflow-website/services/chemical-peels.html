Chemical Peels

From $185 90 min

A professional chemical peel gently exfoliates the outer skin layers to promote cell turnover, smooth texture, even tone, and reveal a clearer, brighter complexion. Auroraflow's peel protocol is safe for all skin tones, including dark and melanin-rich skin, and is always preceded by a clinical consultation. Requires the Clinical Facial & Consultation as a prerequisite.

Book Appointment Not sure? Book a free consultation

Good For

  • Uneven skin tone & hyperpigmentation
  • Fine lines & texture
  • Acne & post-acne scars
  • Dull, rough skin
  • All skin tones including skin of color

What to Expect

  • Clinical consultation before first peel
  • Chemical solution applied & neutralized
  • Mild tingling or warmth during treatment
  • Possible light peeling in following days

How to Prepare

  • Clinical Facial consultation required first
  • Discontinue retinoids 7+ days before
  • No waxing or laser treatments 2 weeks before
  • Commit to daily SPF 30+ after treatment
