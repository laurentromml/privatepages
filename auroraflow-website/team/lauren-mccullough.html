Lauren McCullough

Co-founder

Lauren McCullough is the co-founder of Auroraflow. She and her partner Castle Frame built Auroraflow from the ground up with one core belief: that wellness is a fundamental right, not a luxury, and that for too long, the wellness industry has built spaces that feel unwelcoming to queer people, BIPOC clients, plus-size bodies, and anyone who doesn't fit the narrow mold of who "spa culture" has traditionally been designed for.

Every decision at Auroraflow — from the way the team is trained, to the intake forms used, to the way services are priced — has been made with the goal of radical inclusion. That means pronouns on nametags, tables with no weight limits, services priced by time rather than gender, and Pay What You Can days every other Thursday.

Lauren is proudly based in downtown Durham, NC, a city as diverse and vibrant as the community Auroraflow serves every day.
