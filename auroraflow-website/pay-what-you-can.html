Every 2nd & 4th Thursday of the month

Wellness for
everyone.

We know that in the current American environment, massage and professional wellness services are often treated as luxuries, things you do when you can afford them. We fundamentally disagree with that framing.

Stress affects everyone. Bodies need care. And the people who most need a moment of rest and healing are often the ones who can least afford it.

Pay What You Can days are our commitment to changing that, even just a little, right here in Durham. Spots are limited, so we ask that if you are able to pay full price, you do. That generosity helps us keep this program running for those who truly need it.

How it works

01

Check the schedule

02

Why we do this

Stress doesn't discriminate

Chronic stress disproportionately affects marginalized communities: BIPOC individuals, low-income families, and those with less economic security. We believe that access to healing shouldn't follow the same inequities.

Community is our why

Auroraflow was built by and for the Durham community. Pay What You Can days are one concrete way we put that value into practice, not just as a statement, but as a recurring commitment.

It takes all of us

This program works because clients who can pay full price or more help subsidize access for those who can't. Every time you choose Auroraflow, you're part of that ecosystem of community care.

No questions asked

We don't ask you to prove financial need, fill out a form, or explain yourself. You decide what to pay. We trust you. That trust is part of what makes this space feel different.
