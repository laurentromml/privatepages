Community Classes

Move with us,
grow with us.

An ever-evolving lineup of group sessions, shaped by what our practitioners love to teach, and what our community asks us to bring next.

A program that keeps growing.

Our community classes are designed to extend the care of our private sessions into spaces where people can practice together, without prerequisites, without performance.

Some classes return season after season; others are one-time gatherings led by visiting practitioners. The lineup shifts, but the spirit doesn't.
