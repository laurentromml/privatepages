PSKB Overview

Your reps work hard. Now you can make that work visible to vendors and management.

Minecart makes it easy to plan, prep, and capture what your reps are already doing every day, across every line. Report Studio turns that into reports your vendor and distributor partners actually use, for better pull-through and a relationship that scales.

Managing an aftermarket sales team is a visibility problem

  • The best reps hate data entry, so the best information never gets written down.
  • Managers find out what happened in the field weeks later, if ever.
  • When a rep retires or leaves, the account history walks out the door with them.

For a rep agency, it compounds

  • One shop conversation covers five lines. Without a system, only the loudest line gets credit.
  • A handful of vendors require monthly reports, and one of your partners compiles them by hand. The rest of your vendors see nothing at all.
  • Line retention is decided above your day-to-day contacts. When a sales manager moves on, the people upstairs ask what the check buys.
  • Most of the real work happens in Outlook, and none of it gets counted.

What Minecart does for an agency

  • Run the team without ride-alongs. See every rep's activity as it happens, verify line coverage, and know whether the campaign actually got mentioned in the field.
  • Make vendors stickier, then bigger. Executive-ready monthly reports for every line, not just the ones that demand them. Proof of work is how you defend lines and win bigger territories.
  • Own the intelligence. Every note becomes PSKB property. Territories transfer with their history attached.

More time in the right places

  • The day plans itself. Reps open the app to a priority queue, accounts that need attention, sorted by distance, instead of guessing over coffee where to go first.
  • Campaign targets track themselves. If a rep is behind pace on mentioning a new line or launch, Minecart flags it before the vendor has to ask why.
  • No more paperwork at night. The one-minute note in the truck is the call report. Hours back every week, not spent typing.

How it works

  1. Capture. Rep finishes a stop, hits one button, talks for a minute. No forms, works offline. Our best users are in their 60s.
  2. Sort. AI tags the account and splits the note by line, so every vendor mentioned gets credit. The same notes brief the rep before the next visit.
  3. Report. Month end, the vendor report drafts itself in Report Studio. A partner reviews, edits, approves. Nothing goes out without you.
